# Activity 1.4 – Social or Business Impact

## Social Impact

Insurance fraud negatively affects society by increasing premium costs for genuine customers. When fraudulent claims are approved, insurance companies suffer financial losses, which ultimately impacts honest policyholders.

By implementing a Machine Learning-based fraud detection system:

- Fraudulent claims can be reduced.
- Genuine customers receive fair and timely claim settlements.
- Trust between customers and insurance companies improves.
- Transparency in claim processing increases.

This promotes fairness and accountability in the insurance ecosystem.

---

## Business Impact

Insurance fraud causes significant financial losses and increases operational costs due to manual investigation processes.

By using an automated fraud detection system:

- Financial losses can be minimized.
- Claim processing becomes faster and more efficient.
- High-risk claims can be flagged for investigation.
- Operational costs are reduced.
- Decision-making becomes data-driven.

Overall, the system enhances profitability, efficiency, and risk management for insurance companies.
